# Restrictions

This repository is a private staging area for a public-safe static website. It must stay free of private, confidential, unsupported, or operationally sensitive material.

The public surface is limited to:

- `/` as the short balanced two-track public hub
- `/procurement/` as the Deal Hunter procurement intelligence track
- `/ai-control/` as the AI Control and evidence systems track
- `/pt/` as the Portuguese (`pt-PT`) short balanced two-track public hub
- `/pt/procurement/` as the Portuguese (`pt-PT`) Deal Hunter procurement intelligence track
- `/pt/ai-control/` as the Portuguese (`pt-PT`) AI Control and evidence systems track

English is the default language. Portuguese pages are translations under `/pt/`, not separate strategic surfaces or product launches. Language switching must stay static and map only to equivalent pages. Do not add browser-language detection, redirects, JavaScript language logic, cookies, or local storage for language state.

Do not commit:

- source code from other products
- PDFs or raw research exports
- private screenshots
- customer, client, or company logos
- credentials, tokens, keys, `.env` files, or deployment secrets
- IBM, client, employer, or partner confidential material
- customer data, customer names, testimonials, or traction claims
- TrustGate media release binaries, production metadata, TGVIDEO metadata, TG360-EVID IDs, CACSAC phase IDs, or CP-7 artifacts
- private product repositories or internal repository names
- implementation internals
- source maps, scoring formulas, proof schemas, or country-source implementation recipes
- TrustGate protocol internals
- TrustGate Sovereign proof internals, internal evidence IDs, or execution-control implementation details
- Deal Hunter Portugal alpha logic
- private go-to-market notes
- raw pasted PDF source material

Do not claim:

- customers or deployments
- production readiness
- production execution service status
- production security gateway status
- compliance certification or compliance guarantee
- legal determination
- Portuguese compliance, legal, production, regulatory approval, or customer guarantees
- IBM endorsement, IBM partnership, IBM Engineering AI Hub integration, live watsonx Orchestrate integration, live Manta integration, or live Db2 integration
- live telemetry, connector execution, CP-7 authorization, ROI, realized savings, guaranteed savings, or avoided liability
- unqualified execution-firewall positioning, production enforcement, or enterprise-wide non-bypassability
- DORA compliance or certification, GDPR compliance, complete regulatory coverage, or supervisory acceptance
- public receipt schemas, provenance mechanics, target-verification implementation, receipt-consumption implementation, or deployment topology
- prediction, win probability, or likely winner selection
- autonomous decisioning
- replacement for human review
- solver validation, safety certification, or automatic design approval

Do not make the homepage a long portfolio page. Do not make either homepage track visually overpower the other. Do not bury the Boundaries trust signal; each page must expose its own `#boundaries` section and navigation link.

Do not add analytics, cookies, contact forms, tracking pixels, newsletter signup, external JavaScript, external CSS, external fonts, or third-party embeds without a privacy and publication review.

Do not make this private staging repo public if it ever contains rejected drafts, private notes, sensitive files, or private history. Publish by copying reviewed public-safe files into a fresh public repository.
